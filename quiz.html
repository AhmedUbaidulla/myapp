Basic Naming Rules

1. Carbon Prefix

  • 1 carbon → Meth
  • 2 carbons → Eth
  • 3 carbons → Prop
  • 4 carbons → But
  • 5 carbons → Pent
  • 6 carbons → Hex

2. Functional Groups

  • Alkane → -ane
  • Alkene → -ene
  • Alcohol → -ol

Examples

CH₄ → Methane

C₂H₆ → Ethane

C₃H₆ → Propene

C₂H₅OH → Ethanol
